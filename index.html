Fast. Really Fast.

Ra1n0ver is a snappy, responsive experience that you can't find anywhere else, with support for all iOS versions from iOS 13.0-13.7, including A12 & A13 devices.

All Open Source

Ra1n0ver is completely open source, written almost entirely in Swift and welcomes community contributions and pull requests, Open source should be avaible in the near future

All new. All improved.

Ra1n0ver comes with the open source Procursus bootstrap, designed from the ground up with openness and compatiblity in mind. Ra1n0ver also comes equipped with full libhooker support, so speed and reliabilty are ensured.
